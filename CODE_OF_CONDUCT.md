# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and considerate in communication.
- Give and accept constructive feedback.
- Assume positive intent and collaborate in good faith.
- Focus on what is best for the community and project.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, or insulting language.
- Public or private intimidation, threats, or stalking.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Any conduct that is inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior. Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces, including issue trackers, pull requests, discussions, and community channels related to this repository.

## Reporting

To report unacceptable behavior, open a private security report through GitHub Security Advisories or contact the maintainers directly at `security@saga.co.id`.

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
